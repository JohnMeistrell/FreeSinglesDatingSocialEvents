Who:

Ages 50 to 70ish with old-fashioned relationship values.

You may bring one support friend whom may not be interested in dating. If you do, please ask the host for wings stickers so all will know that your friend is your wing man/woman and they are not looking to date.

Why and How:

Singles events are good for making friends, but very few who attend are looking to date. Dating sites seem like a good idea (yeah right). You are reading this so I don't need to say any more.

After much thought and many conversations, we have structured our events to have strategies similar to those used by popular dating sites and speed dating events. Dating site matches are based on information that clients have provided and what clients are looking for. Popular speed dating events have fun ice-breaking activites, and mixers with real people who are looking for fun and conversation.

We will start with one of the popular selections: Old-fashioned relationship values, a slow paced walk and optional dinner/coffee, and add more activies and places as our group grows. We combine best of both and keep it simple, consistent, and for most events $free.
